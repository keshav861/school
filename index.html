Why the SMAPS

We at “SMAPS” are committed to provide quality and modern infrastructure, continuing realignment of curriculum and committed and skilled faculty. At “SMAPS” with the help of personal attention your child will be groomed to become a confident and enlightened youth, ready to step into global society with honour, dignity and integrity.

• Personal attention, where your child gets an opportunity to outshine his caliber.
• A complete school, starting from play group to 10+2
• Exclusive, well equipped junior wing with indoor auditorium.
• Multimedia/smart classrooms.
• Centrally air-conditioned and spacious school building.
• A rich library, well equipped science and computer laboratories.
• Horse Riding “Dance and Music Room”.

Unique at SMAPS


• The school provides special coaching from class-5th onwards, which prepare them for competitions like IIT-JEE, NEE, NDA, Sainik School, NTSE, Olympiad etc.

• Best academic Board Results from last decade.

Exclusive Junior Wing

The kindergarten wing at New India is the first rung of the ladder in the development of the child. Cheerful interiors and specially designed furniture clubbed with individual attention given by teachers make the wing a perfect heaven for learning and laughter. The teachers strive to give each child a learning experience that is joyful, effortless and full of fun. The Play room of tiny tots is full of exciting games and educative. The tiny tots feel at home by story-telling session and by viewing educative movies, rhymes, P.P.T's etc. on the smart board. "Splash pool" and Sant Pit" are enjoyed by the children do in free time.
